Please note the following important information:

Option A: Application Payments:

If you have previously applied and would now like to pay the Application Fee please proceed to make payment via the Application Portal. Click here to access the Application Portal. Select the relevant application form and enter your Application ID previously received. You will require a credit card or internet banking (EFT) to make payment via this portal. If you would like to make an EFT please ensure that your card is registered for online purchases and that you have internet banking – select OZOW and your appropriate bank when making payment. If you would like to use a credit card – please ensure that your card has been registered for the Online Payments - 3D Secure service - click here to read how to register your card for this service. If your card is not registered for this service this will result in a failed transaction.

Option B: Registered Student Payments:

If you are a Registered Student and would like to make a payment - please click here to access the payment page. Please enter your Student Number as a reference. You will require a credit card or internet banking (EFT) to make payment via this portal. If you would like to make an EFT please ensure that your card is registered for online purchases and that you have internet banking – select OZOW and your appropriate bank when making payment. If you would like to use a credit card – please ensure that your card has been registered for the Online Payments - 3D Secure service - click here to read how to register your card for this service. If your card is not registered for this service – this will result in a failed transaction.

Option C:

International Student Payments: If you are an international student and do not have a South African bank account - please click here!

For Options A and B, if you do not have a credit card or are not registered for internet banking, you will then need to make payment directly at your bank via a direct deposit – please allow additional time for processing if this method is selected.

Please note that in order to process your Full-Time and Part-Time application a non-refundable fee of R400 is required for full-time applications and R250 for Part-Time (contact) and Distance applications. Please note that a limited number of students can be accepted to programmes at The IIE’s Varsity College, each academic year.
Once payment has been made a Student Advisor will then contact you to complete the application process. Applications for 2nd Semester part-time study open on 1 April 2021 and are accepted on a first come basis. Applications for 2022 open on 1 April.
